📘 GIẢI BÀI TẬP WORKBOOK VOICES Intermediate Plus | UNITS 1 → 5

🔥 Học vẹt + nhận diện nhanh — Các từ được in đậm là dấu hiệu nhận biết giúp bạn chọn đáp án mà không cần hiểu sâu.
🔷 UNIT 1: YOUR LIFE
📖 GRAMMAR (trang 6-7)
📌 Exercise 1: Sắp xếp câu hỏi
1. Do you remember my friend Jackie?
2. Isn't Wayan's brother living in Indonesia?
3. Haven't you made some new friends at work?
4. Didn't Wang grow up in China?
5. Are your oldest friends still living in your hometown?
6. Have you met Sylvie's friend from Krakow?
📌 Exercise 2: Nối câu hỏi - câu trả lời
1. Do you remember → c. Yes, I do.
2. Isn't → f. No, he isn't.
3. Haven't → d. No, I haven't yet.
4. Didn't → e. Yes, he did.
5. Are → b. No, they aren't.
6. Have → a. Yes, I have. She's lovely.
📌 Exercise 3: Chọn câu trả lời đúng
1. We've known for 20 years → B. You have?
2. I didn't know → A. Neither did I.
3. Did you become friends? → C. Yes, we did.
4. You didn't see → D. B and C are correct
5. I met Karla → A. She's from there, isn't she?
6. Can you give me... → B. Didn't I already give it to you?
7. Piet and Carmen met → A. They met at work, didn't they?
📌 Exercise 4: Điền trợ động từ
1. have  2. haven't  3. aren't  4. don't  5. do
6. Did  7. (you?)  8. was  9. were  10. do
📌 Exercise 5: Sắp xếp hội thoại
1g → 2a → 3h → 4b → 5f → 6c → 7i → 8j → 9d → 10e
📌 Exercise 6: Điền một từ
1. Do  2. Yes  3. is  4. No  5. has  6. Neither  7. is  8. do
🔷 UNIT 2: BREAKING THE RULES
📖 GRAMMAR (trang 14-15)
📌 Exercise 1: Chọn dạng đúng của động từ
1. happened  2. noticed  3. hadn't written
4. looked  5. appeared  6. was using
7. did you do  8. reported
9. happened  10. was looking  11. changed
📌 Exercise 2: Sắp xếp câu hỏi
1. What were they doing when he called?
2. Had you heard of the company before they contacted you?
3. Were they expecting the package when it arrived?
4. Where were you working when you met Dimitri?
5. Hadn't you used the credit card for online shopping?
6. Did they ask whether it had been stolen?
7. Was she using her laptop when she was hacked?
8. Why hadn't you locked the door before you left?
📌 Exercise 3: Chia động từ (QKĐ, QKTD, QKHT)
1. heard  2. was working  3. had finished
4. was looking  5. didn't have  6. said
7. lost  8. had saved  9. learned
🔷 UNIT 3: IMAGINING THE FUTURE
📖 GRAMMAR (trang 24-25)
📌 Exercise 1 & 2: Chọn đáp án SAI và sửa
1. ❌ probably going to work → ✅ will probably work
2. ❌ doing jobs → ✅ do jobs
3. ❌ probably control → ✅ will probably control
4. ❌ more electricity is needed → ✅ more electricity will be needed
5. ❌ a better solution will be invented → ✅ a better solution is invented
6. ❌ have built → ✅ will have built
📌 Exercise 3: Chia động từ (will/going to + thì HT trong mệnh đề thời gian)
1. is (by the time he is 21)
2. ends (After the cold weather ends)
3. get (As soon as I get a job)
4. won't leave (She won't leave unless she finds)
5. will probably find
6. will feel
📌 Exercise 4: Chọn liên từ thời gian
1. as soon as  2. unless  3. By the time
4. once  5. When  6. after
🔷 UNIT 4: GOOD TASTE
📖 GRAMMAR (trang 32-33)
📌 Exercise 1: Hoàn thành câu với từ so sánh
1. b. by far the oldest
2. g. much older than
3. a. a bit older than
4. f. as old as
5. c. more and more popular
6. e. far more easily than
7. g. much older than
📌 Exercise 2: Thêm từ vào câu
1. Pho is one of Vietnam's most famous foods.
2. He's cooking at home more regularly now.
3. We have rice much more often than pasta.
4. She likes her coffee a bit sweeter than I do.
5. This is by far the best lunch I've had.
📌 Exercise 3: Chọn cụm từ thay thế
1. is less spicy than
2. a bit
3. much
4. by far
5. much
📌 Exercise 4: Viết lại câu (nghĩa tương tự)
1. This mango is the sweetest I've ever had.
2. White rice is much more common than brown rice.
3. There are more and more sushi restaurants in town.
4. The soup isn't as spicy as I expected it to be.
5. I was eating more slowly than Helena and Dave.
